Our Services

Crop Management

Manage and monitor crop details such as crop name, type, and sowing date. All crop data is stored securely using Google Sheets, making it easy to view and update records anytime.

Weather Monitoring

Get real-time weather information including temperature, humidity, and weather conditions using a live weather API. This helps farmers make better decisions based on current climate conditions.

Irrigation Scheduling

Receive simple irrigation recommendations based on crop type and weather data. This helps in efficient water usage and prevents over- or under-irrigation.

Yield Estimation

Estimate crop yield using basic calculations based on crop type, land area, and average weather conditions. This feature helps farmers plan harvesting and market supply.
